Meta

Meta owns four of the largest social media platforms, each with over one billion monthly active users.

I joined Meta as the design lead for the Facebook Incubator. I oversaw design for all Incubator products, developed 0-1 strategies, promoted best practices, and mentored colleagues across disciplines. I collaborated vertically with pillars to identify and validate new incubation opportunities; horizontally on the design leadership council to staff and ship the highest priority projects. I led two major initiatives, managed multiple sprints, and joined a hackathon. My efforts doubled design system adoption and improved product quality.

Quality

  • Product Manager
  • Principal Designer
  • Designed with John MacMenamin

I defined new metrics that enabled us to quantify quality. I moved the needle by evangelizing best practices, incorporating linting into design reviews, and automating the bug filing process. These changes incentivized colleagues to leverage existing components and more actively participate in bug reporting during alphas/betas. My efforts increased design quality by 100%.

To further boost quality improvements, I built an extension to the Facebook Design System to power a new Figma starter file. This empowered colleagues to create internally branded Figma projects with consistent structures, flows, and statuses in a matter of seconds.
